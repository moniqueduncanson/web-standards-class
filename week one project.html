South America

South America is home to many countries, cultures and wildlife. we are going to take a look at some of the different life styles as we take tour in one of the most amazing places in the world.

Brazil

when you think of Brazil you think live music,dancing, different foods, lifestyles traditions and customs. but this country is much more then that. known for being the largest country in South America.Its population is 198.7 million and that was recorded in 2012. but dont let that stop you. this country have much to offer with its historical feel and great history. lets just say there is alway something to do here. one of the most beloved is Rio de Janeiro located in southeastern Brazil. Rio de Janeiro is the most visited city of south America due to is beaches, famous mountains, landmarks and carnival festival.

Colombia

Colombia bursts with natural beauty and cultural treasures it is a beautiful country that offers everything a tourist could dream of. from the ancient ruins, amazon jungles, Caribbean Beaches sown-capped peaks and beautifully preserved colonial towns. One of the best places to go in Colombia is Cartagena. whether you're there to wander through the narrow streets of the world heritage listed old city, party or chill out on the beaches of amazing neighboring islands. Cartagena certainly have something for everyone.

Peru

Peru is one place you can never say that you didn't learn something new when you went there. Being one of the most historical countries in South America. Peru is one of the great centers of ancient civilization, but no one said you need to be a history buff ro enjoy Peru. There are some great natural wonders, also there is the arid strip from the lush amazon rainforest proving a diverse range of travel experience.

As you can see South America is full of everything for anyone it has it all for families, singles, friends, couples. no matter what your group south America has it. so dont wait for tomorrow or when it to late that a trip to South America and see what they can offer you.
